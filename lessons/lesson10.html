Lesson 10: Ultrasonic Sensors

Introduction

Ultrasonic sensors provide an effective way for robots to detect obstacles and measure distances. In this lesson, we'll focus specifically on ultrasonic sensors, how they work, and how to implement them in robotic projects for navigation and obstacle avoidance.
