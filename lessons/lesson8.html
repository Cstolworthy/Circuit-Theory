Lesson 8: Sensors & Serial Communication

Introduction

Sensors are the eyes and ears of robotic systems, allowing them to perceive their environment. Serial communication enables robots to exchange data with other devices and systems. In this lesson, we'll explore various sensors and how to use serial communication to transmit sensor data.
