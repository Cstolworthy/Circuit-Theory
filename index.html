Circuit Theory & Robotics

A comprehensive guide to understanding circuits and building robots

Course Overview

Welcome to our comprehensive course on Circuit Theory and Robotics! This course is designed to take you from the basics of number systems all the way to building and programming your own robot. Each lesson builds upon the previous one, providing a solid foundation in electronics, circuits, and robotics.

Whether you're a beginner curious about electronics or someone looking to advance your skills in robotics, this course offers detailed explanations, practical activities, and hands-on projects to enhance your learning experience.

Course Curriculum

Lesson 1: Number Systems

Introduction to binary, decimal, and hexadecimal number systems used in computing and electronics.

Lesson 2: Binary Arithmetic

Learn to perform addition, subtraction, and other operations in binary.

Lesson 3: Logic Gates

Understand the basic building blocks of digital circuits: AND, OR, NOT, and more.

Lesson 4: Circuit Components

Explore resistors, capacitors, transistors, and other fundamental components.

Lesson 5: Breadboard Basics

Learn how to use breadboards for circuit prototyping.

Lesson 6: LEDs & Digital Outputs

Control LEDs and understand digital outputs in circuits.

Lesson 7: Buttons & Switches

Implement user input through buttons and various switches.

Lesson 8: Sensors & Serial Communication

Use sensors to gather data and transmit it through serial communication.

Lesson 9: Motor Control

Learn to control DC motors, servo motors, and stepper motors.

Lesson 10: Ultrasonic Sensors

Implement distance measurement using ultrasonic sensors.

Lesson 11: Combining Sensors & Motors

Create reactive systems by integrating sensors with motor control.

Lesson 12: Assembling the Robot

Put together all components to build your robot's physical structure.

Lesson 13: Writing the Code

Program your robot to perform tasks and respond to its environment.

Lesson 14: Testing & Debugging

Test your robot and learn how to identify and fix issues.

Lesson 15: Enhancements & Creativity

Take your robot to the next level with advanced features and creative additions.
